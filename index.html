// FAQ

Common Questions

What is Manifest Trade? +
Manifest Trade is a cross-chain bridge and DEX aggregator for EVM networks. It enables users to swap and bridge assets between 12+ blockchains in under 40 seconds, with MPC + ZK security and a 0.1% flat fee.
How fast are bridge transfers? +
Average bridge time is 36 seconds. Times vary by destination chain: BSC ~25s, Solana ~22s, Ethereum ~45s, Polygon ~30s. All are significantly faster than traditional bridges.
How is my transfer secured? +
Manifest Trade uses MPC for key management across 15+ nodes and Zero-Knowledge Proofs for cross-chain state verification. Audited by CertiK, PeckShield, Hacken, and Trail of Bits.
What are the fees? +
A flat 0.1% bridge fee on all transfers. No hidden costs. EVM network gas fees apply separately, but are minimal on most supported chains.
What assets can I bridge? +
Supported assets include ETH, USDT, DAI, BNB, MATIC, SOL, and many popular ERC-20 and BEP-20 tokens. More assets are added regularly through governance.
